Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation? +
Browse our categories, select an expert, choose your preferred consultation type (chat, call, or video), pick a time slot, and pay securely. It's that simple!
Are the experts verified? +
Yes, every expert on Tapanta goes through a thorough verification process. We check their qualifications, experience, and credentials before they can offer consultations.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed securely.
Can I get a refund? +
Yes, if an expert doesn't show up or you're unsatisfied with the consultation, you can request a refund within 24 hours. Our team will review and process it promptly.
How do I become an expert on Tapanta? +
Fill out the "Join as Expert" form with your details, qualifications, and experience. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 48 hours.
