About Tapanta

Our mission is to make expert knowledge accessible to everyone

Tapanta was born from a simple observation: millions of people struggle to find reliable, personalized advice when they need it most. Whether it's a career decision, a health concern, a legal question, or a business challenge — the right guidance can change everything.

Instead of searching Google for hours, watching unreliable YouTube videos, or paying exorbitant fees for professional consultations, Tapanta connects you directly with verified experts who have real experience in their fields.

Our platform brings together doctors, lawyers, chartered accountants, psychologists, fitness trainers, business consultants, career coaches, nutritionists, astrologers, startup founders, and teachers — all in one place, accessible at your fingertips.

For experts, Tapanta provides a platform to monetize their knowledge, reach a wider audience, and help people from anywhere in the world. We believe that expertise should be shared, not hoarded.
